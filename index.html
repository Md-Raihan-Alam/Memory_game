Read Me!!!
This is a memory game. You will have 2 second to memorize all images position and then all picture will turn around. Now you will have to turn them around by clicking on them. You have to match pair. If you match pairs you will get 3 points and for a wrong pair you will get -2. The time will matter as well. With each time you will get -0.01. The game will go on until you find all the pairs. After completing the game you will get to save your score and name
